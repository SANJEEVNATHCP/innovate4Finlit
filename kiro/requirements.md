## Problem Statement
Women managing household and income finances often lack digital confidence and long-term planning skills, leading to unsafe financial practices and limited financial security.

## Functional Requirements
- Voice-guided financial scenarios
- Decision-based gameplay (no quizzes)
- Budgeting and savings simulations
- Digital finance and fraud-awareness scenarios
- XP, levels, badges, and Resilience Score
- Offline gameplay with progress sync
- Visual, low-text interface

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Low-bandwidth and lightweight
- Rural and low-literacy friendly
- Secure user data handling
- Scalable to large user base

## Hackathon Constraints Compliance
- Financial Themes: Budgeting, Savings, Digital Finance, Fraud Prevention
- Behavior Over Theory: Learning through decisions and consequences
- Rural-Ready Technology: Voice-first, offline-first, visual UI
